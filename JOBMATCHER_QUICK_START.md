# JobMatcher Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Get Started in 2 Minutes

### Step 1: Open JobMatcher
Navigate to the **Job Matcher** page from the main menu.

### Step 2: Select Your Resume
1. Click **"Auto-Search Jobs"** section
2. Choose your resume/CV from the dropdown
3. Location auto-detects (or set manually)
4. Set search radius (default: 30 miles)

### Step 3: Search for Jobs
```
Option A: Leave blank → searches based on your current job title
Option B: Enter specific search → "Senior React Engineer", "Product Manager", etc.
```
Then click **"Auto-Search Jobs"** button.

### Step 4: Wait & Review
The system searches all major job boards:
- ✅ LinkedIn
- ✅ Indeed  
- ✅ Glassdoor
- ✅ ZipRecruiter
- ✅ Dice (Tech)
- ✅ FlexJobs (Remote)
- ✅ WellFound (Startups)
- ✅ Upwork (Freelance)
- ✅ Company Career Pages

Results appear ranked by how well they match YOUR background.

---

## 📊 Understanding the Scores

### Two Important Scores

**1. Job Fit Score (left side, big number)**
- How well you match the job requirements
- 85+ = Excellent (high match)
- 70-84 = Good (solid candidate)
- 50-69 = Fair (might need to learn)
- <50 = Stretch (might be difficult)

**2. Background Fit (shown below company name)**
- How this job fits your career path
- 70%+ = Great next step
- 50-69% = Possible move
- <50% = Different direction

## 🔍 Finding Your Perfect Match

### Filter Results
Use the filter bar to narrow down:
- **Score Range** - Only show 70+ scores
- **Location Type** - Remote only
- **Job Source** - Just LinkedIn results
- **Min Score** - Only 75+ matches

### Sort Results  
- **Score (High to Low)** → Best matches first
- **Background Fit (High)** → Best career moves
- **Date (Newest)** → Recently posted

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Be Specific with Searches**
   - "Senior Software Engineer" > "Engineer"
   - Better results with exact titles

2. **Use Background Fit Score**
   - Prioritize jobs where background fit is 60%+
   - These align with your career

3. **Check Multiple Sources**
   - Glassdoor for company reviews
   - LinkedIn for network connections
   - Indeed for volume
   - Dice for tech roles

4. **Location Matters**
   - Remote jobs float to top when searched
   - Set radius if you prefer in-person
   - Both remote and local options available

5. **Save Your Favorites**
   - Click "Full Analysis" to move to Interested
   - Go to **Application Tracker** to manage applications

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Why are some scores low even though I match?**
A: The AI is conservative - it only counts skills/experience you explicitly mention. Add more details to your resume.

**Q: Which source will have the best jobs?**
A: Depends on your field:
- Tech → Dice, LinkedIn, Indeed
- Startups → WellFound
- Remote → FlexJobs
- Freelance → Upwork
- All sectors → LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor

**Q: Can I search without auto-selecting location?**
A: Yes! Enter "Remote" for remote jobs anywhere.

**Q: How often should I search?**
A: Job boards update daily. Search:
- Weekly for active job hunting
- When changing titles
- When open to new opportunities

**Q: Can I apply directly from here?**
A: Click "View Job" to go to original listing and apply directly. Or use "Quick Apply" to add to your **Application Tracker** for follow-up.

## 🚨 Troubleshooting

### No results found
**Try:**
- Different search term (broader)
- "Remote" as location
- Lower score filter
- Check your resume is complete

### Low match scores
**Your resume might be:**
- Missing specific keywords
- Not highlighting relevant experience
- Outdated or incomplete
- **Solution:** Update resume, try again

### Same job from multiple sources
**This is normal!** Popular jobs appear on multiple boards.
- System automatically removes exact duplicates
- Review once, dismiss if not interested

### Search taking too long
**System timeout is 45-60 seconds**
- If still loading after that, refresh page
- Try simpler search term
- Check internet connection
