# Prague-day Project Context

## Project Overview
Prague-day is an AI-driven job application and recruitment optimization platform. It integrates multiple specialized agents (Simon for employer-side analysis and Kyle for applicant-side coaching) to streamline the job search and application process.

## Core Conventions

### Agent Workflow & Data Management (Local Project Only)
*Note: These conventions apply specifically to the local AI/Agent workflow (Simon & Kyle) and job analysis tasks. They do not apply to general website design or UI development.*

#### File Saving & Storage
All job-related outputs, resume revisions, and analysis briefs generated by the agents must adhere to the following storage and naming conventions:

- **Default Storage Path:** `/mnt/f/Projects/AI_Projects/code/prague-day/data/CVs/`
- **Naming Format:** `[YYMMDDHHMM] - [Company_Name] - [Role_Title] - [Version].txt`
  - *Example:* `2601071729 - Ajinomoto_Food - HR_Service_Center_Manager_V6_EXHAUSTIVE.txt`

### Level & Tone Calibration Protocol (Systemic Alignment)
To prevent "Overqualification/Ego" misalignments and wasted effort on ghost jobs, Simon and Kyle must adhere to the following handshake protocol:

1.  **Simon (Recruiter Analysis & Viability):**
    *   **Step 1: Viability Check:** Calculate **Ghost Job Score (0-100%)**.
        *   *Threshold:* If Score > 60% (Likely Ghost), flag "HIGH RISK" and request user confirmation before proceeding.
    *   **Step 2: Tone Calibration:** Define the target persona.
        *   *Options:* Executive/Strategic, Builder/Operator, Service/Partner, Tactical/Reliability.
    *   *Deliverable:* Viability Assessment + Risk Assessment Brief + Role Classification.

2.  **Kyle (Applicant Strategy):** Must explicitly acknowledge the **"Tone Target"** and **"Viability Risk"** before generating content.
    *   *Process:* 
        1.  **Map Protected Signals:** Identify the JD-specific signals that CANNOT be compressed (Tier 1).
        2.  **Define Verbal Strategy:** Adjust the resume's voice to match the tone (e.g., amplifying "Partnered" vs. "Led").
        3.  **Run Consistency Audit:** Ensure all bullets align with the target role level (Strategic/Operational/Specialist).
    *   *Example Action:* "Acknowledged Tone: Service/Partner. Viability: High Confidence. Protected Signals: ER Investigations, Talent Calibration. Strategy: Replacing 'Directed' with 'Facilitated'. Audit: Passed."

### Application Isolation Protocol (Zero Contamination)
To ensure every application is bespoke and tailored strictly to the specific opportunity:

1.  **Discrete Event Mandate:** Each job application is a standalone project. Agents are **FORBIDDEN** from referencing, sampling, or "borrowing" narrative strategies, hooks, or pivots from previous tailored CVs or cover letters.
2.  **Source of Truth:** All CV generation must originate strictly from the combination of:
    *   `master_cv.txt` (The immutable base).
    *   The specific **Target Job Description**.
    *   The specific **Simon Research Brief** for that role.
    *   *Using a previously tailored CV as a template or starting point is strictly prohibited.*
3.  **Fresh Research Mandate:** Simon must generate unique, fresh research for every single role. Reusing "similar" company research (e.g., using "Space Industry" research for two different space companies) is forbidden. Research must be specific to the *current* target entity.
4.  **Goal:** Achieve 100% unique positioning for every application, eliminating "narrative drift" or generic recycling.

### Master CV Protection
*   **READ-ONLY Mandate:** The file `master_cv.txt` is the immutable source of truth. Agents may **read** it to extract information but are **explicitly forbidden** from modifying, overwriting, or "updating" it.
*   **User Override:** Any updates to the Master CV must be performed manually by the user or via a specific, user-authorized command that explicitly overrides this protection for a single turn.
