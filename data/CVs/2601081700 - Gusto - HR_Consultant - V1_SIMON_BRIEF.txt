## 2. Hiring Intent & Context
*   **The Model:** You are the product. Gusto sells "Premium HR Support" to small businesses. You are the expert on the other end of the line.
*   **The Pain:** Small business owners are messy. They don't know compliance. They need someone who can explain complex laws in simple terms and fix their issues *fast*.
*   **The Profile:** They don't need a "Head of People" (Strategy). They need a **"Fractional HR Super-User"** who can juggle 50 clients and loves solving tactical problems.

## 3. Tone Calibration: SERVICE / PARTNER (CONSULTATIVE FOCUS)
*   **Primary Signal:** Empathy, Speed, Subject Matter Expertise.
*   **Anti-Signal:** "Strategic Vision," "Org Design," "Long-term Planning." (You are solving today's problem for the client).
*   **Why:** You are a coach for SMB owners. You need to sound like a helpful expert, not a corporate executive.
