## 🎯 Integration Overview

### Simon (Recruiting & HR Expert) v2.1.0
- **What it does:** Analyzes job opportunities
- **Main functions:**
  - `AI.analyzeJob()` - Full job analysis
  - `AI.checkGhostJob()` - Ghost job detection
  - `AI.classifyRole()` - Role classification
- **Knowledge files:** 6 files covering job analysis, ghost jobs, etc.

### Kyle (CV & Cover Letter Expert) v2.1.0
- **What it does:** Optimizes resume and application materials
- **Main functions:**
  - `AI.optimizeResume()` - Resume optimization
  - `AI.prepareInterview()` - Interview prep
  - `AI.getCVBestPractices()` - CV strategies
  - `AI.getCoverLetterBestPractices()` - Cover letter strategies
- **Knowledge files:** 7 files covering resume, interviews, etc.

### Complete Workflow
- **Function:** `AI.analyzeAndOptimize()`
- **What it does:** Simon analyzes job → Kyle optimizes resume
- **Returns:** Analysis + optimization or skip recommendation
